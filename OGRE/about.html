About OGRE

OGRE would not exist if it weren't for Rob Douglas, a close friend and professional programmer who wrote the first version of the program and continues to fix it as I break it. This past summer (2013), Heming Ge, a computer science undergraduate at Cornell, did a great job developing OGRE to be as user-friendly and useful as possible. I spend most of my time introducing bugs into the code, but also added the movie-making capability and bundled libraries into self-contained executables.

OGRE is open source. I am continuing to improve it, but hope that as people start to use it and develop their own personal needs, that they will contribute to the code. Whether or not you can help, let me know if there are any features you would like to see added!
