Lunar Eclipse 2015

UTSC

We organized a night of popular science talks and observations for the lunar eclipse at UTSC in September, 2015. Despite cloudy weather, we had an overwhelming turnout, bringing in about 500 interested students and members of the community. We did manage one quick peek at the eclipsed Moon between the clouds, which was met with much cheering and applause!
